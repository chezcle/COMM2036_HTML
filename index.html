Creating Video from Text: Sora

Sora is an AI model that can create realistic and imaginative scenes form text intrsuctions

All videos on this page were generated directly by Sora without modification

We are teaching AI to understand and simulate the physical world in motion, with the goal of training models that help people solve problems that require real-world interaction.

Introducing Sora, our text-to-video model. Sora can generate videos up to a minute long while maintaining visual quality and adherence to the prompt from the users.

Safety Measures Descriptions
Red Teaming Working with domain experts in areas like misinformation, hateful content, and bias who will adversarially test the Sora model.
Misleading Content Detection Developing tools, including a detection classifier, to identify misleading content generated by Sora.
C2PA Metadata Planning to include C2PA (Content Authenticity and Attribution) metadata in the future if Sora is deployed in an OpenAI product.
